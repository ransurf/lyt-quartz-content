## List Callouts
Want even more color?

Activate the plugin "List Callouts" by mgmeyers to see the color below.

- ! came from `- !` = Important
- @ came from `- @` = Heads Up
- $ came from `- $` = Money
- % came from `- %` = Comment
- & came from `- &` = Note
- ? came from `- ?` = Question
- ~ came from `- ~` = Bookmark
